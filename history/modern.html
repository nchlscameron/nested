Modern History

Modern history covers the period from the late 15th century to the present day, including major developments like the Renaissance, Industrial Revolution, and Information Age.

Major Periods

  • Renaissance (14th-17th century)
  • Age of Discovery (15th-18th century)
  • Industrial Revolution (1760-1840)
  • World Wars (1914-1945)
  • Digital Revolution (1950-present)
