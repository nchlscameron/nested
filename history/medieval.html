Medieval History

The Middle Ages or Medieval Period lasted from the 5th to the late 15th century. It began with the fall of the Western Roman Empire and transitioned into the Renaissance.

Key Events

  • Fall of Rome (476 CE)
  • Rise of Islam (7th century)
  • Crusades (1095-1291)
  • Black Death (1347-1351)
  • Hundred Years' War (1337-1453)
